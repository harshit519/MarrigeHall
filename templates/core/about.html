Our Story

Royal Palace began as a dream to create the most beautiful and memorable wedding experiences. What started as a small family business has grown into the region's premier destination for celebrations of all kinds.

Over the past 25+ years, we've hosted thousands of weddings, corporate events, and special celebrations. Each event has been unique, and we've learned that the key to success is attention to detail, personalized service, and creating an atmosphere where dreams come true.
